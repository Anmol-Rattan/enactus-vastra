Timeline Of The Project

It all started with a Dream of Clothing for poor children around our college, IIT Ropar

  • August 2017

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Vastra started almost a year ago by when ENACTUS IIT Ropar Team is surveying the local communities and villages of Rupnagar.We found there are thousands of people living without proper clothing which is the first and basic need for survival

  • Mid October 2017

    Winter was began to approach plains of Punjab

    Our team had did enough research and was ready to help them during the winter

    So Finally Vastra Was launch . Name Vastra means "Cloth"

  • February 2018 :

    Transition to Full Service

    With the greetings of NEW YEAR our team expanded! its reach to Near by Villages of Nanakpura, etc

  • Mid May 2018 :

    Phase Two Expansion- Online Protal

    With the aim os setting a business model targeting the clothing issue, We came up with the idea of ONLINE "CLOTHING BANK"
    Not only the shelter,blood,food is needed but the equally important is the Clothing,

    so a Clothing Bank Protal was launched which will be maintained by our Enterpenuer belonging to same Poor Community taking and Managing the online orders

  • It is a beginning Be Part
    Of Our
    Story!
